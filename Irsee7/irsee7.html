About this conference

  • Conference topics: Combinatorial structures in Galois geometries; Finite Incidence Geometry; Algebraic curves and varieties over finite fields; Geometric and algebraic coding theory; Finite groups and geometries; Algebraic design theory.

  • This conference extends the series of previous meetings which took place at the Isle of Thorns (2000), in Oberwolfach (2001), and in Irsee since 2003.
