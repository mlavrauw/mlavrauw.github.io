Projective Geometry, Coding Theory, Incidence Geometry in GAP

Lectures, exercises, and GAP-code, for the GAP-packages FinInG and GUAVA

Michel Lavrauw

Michel Lavrauw's homepage
Below you can find the slides, gapcode, and exercise sheets of a minicourse I gave at the GAP Days Spring 2025 at the VUB in Brussels. More details of the GAP days can be found on the website of the workshop. The minicourses consisted of lectures and exercise sessions. There were two further minicourses during the gapdays: one by Ilaria Colazzo including an introduction to GAP and focussing on Group Theory in GAP (permutation groups and matrix groups), and one by Bettina Eick on finite p-groups and solvable groups in GAP, on the GAP packages SmallGroups and GrpConst, and on the Lazard correspondence and Lie p-rings in GAP (LiePRing package). The minicourse I gave, included an introduction to the GAP packages FinInG (Finite Incidence Geometry) and GUAVA (Coding Theory), as well as some more advanced features of these GAP packages.
