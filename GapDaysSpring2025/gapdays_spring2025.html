Below you can find the slides, gapcode, and exercise sheets of a minicourse I gave at the GAP Days Spring 2025 at the VUB in Brussels. More details of the GAP days can be found on the website of the workshop. The minicourse consisted of lectures and exercise sessions, and include an introduction to the GAP packages FinInG (Finite Incidence Geometry) and GUAVA (Coding Theory), as well as some more advanced features of these GAP packages.
